KARMA CREAM IS A LOCALLY OWNED

ICE CREAM CAFÉ OPENED IN 2009.

______________________________

When it comes to providing environmentally and socially responsible treats without artificial

chemicals and preservatives, we’ve got you covered! All of our vegan and dairy ice creams,

coffee and tea are certified organic and we bake exclusively vegan from scratch with all-organic

ingredients. Our vegan and vegetarian sandwiches are also prepared with local, non-GMO and

organic ingredients whenever possible.


It doesn’t end with what we serve — there's also how we serve it! Our offerings are served

in glassware or for takeout in plant-based, compostable to-go supplies and we compost our

food waste through Gainesville Compost.


You can be happy knowing you’re not only treating yourself, but doing something good for

people, animals and the planet!

ICE CREAM

______________________________

We offer a rotating selection of 24 certified organic ice cream flavors daily (12 dairy from Blue

Marble© and 12 vegan from Luna and Larry’s Coconut Bliss©). So come in and take your

pick! Make it into a sundae decked out with our vegan toppings, blend it into a milkshake or

sandwich it between our homemade cookies. And guess what—many toppings, like our

dark chocolate hot fudge and coconut milk whipped cream—are made right in house!


All of our ice creams are free from artificial flavors, food coloring, preservatives, pesticides,

herbicides and other harmful chemicals. Our dairy ice cream comes from milk that’s free of

the unnecessary hormones and antibiotics given to cows in conventional dairy operations

and our vegan coconut milk ice creams are made with only the best-sourced organic and fair

trade ingredients!

BAKERY

______________________________

We're not sure how many times we'll say, "it's organic!" on this site, but FYI, all of our

homemade desserts are organic! Even better, all of our chocolate, cocoa, cinnamon and

vanilla is fair trade certified, too!


Not only that, but we bake exclusively vegan, from scratch and in small, humble batches

throughout the day to ensure freshness. With a variety of cookies, brownies, cupcakes and

other goodies, there’s always something great to pair with your cup of coffee or scoop of ice cream.


Like us on Facebook or follow us on Instagram to receive daily updates of what's baking in the kitchen.

ORGANIC + FAIR TRADE COFFEE & ESPRESSO

______________________________

It gets better. We offer a full service espresso bar with a wide range of options

as well as a variety of drink specials. Our coffee is organic, fair trade certified and roasted locally,

right here in Gainesville by Sweetwater Coffee Roasters. Deep Roots, our drip brew

house coffee, is a smooth, sophisticated blend of Ethiopia Yirgacheffe Full City and Nicaragua Full

City Roast. Our espresso, Red Dog, is a single origin Peruvian espresso; creamy, smooth, and

strong-bodied with mellow berry and dark chocolate tones, topped with a rich red crema.

SANDWICHES & WRAPS

______________________________

Our made-to-order sandwiches are crafted with locally baked bread and, whenever possible,

organic and fair trade ingredients. All sandwiches and wraps are vegan.

Tofurkey Reuben

Tofurkey®, sauerkraut, Russian dressing and Field Roast© Chao cheese on local rye bread. Vegan.

Vegan Bacon + Tofurkey Club

Tofurkey®, tempeh “bacon,” greens, tomatoes and vegenaise on toasted wheat bread. Vegan.

Curried Chickpea + “Chicken” Wrap

Breaded “chicken” strips, curried chickpea salad, greens and tomatoes in a sprouted wheat wrap. Vegan.

Vegan Grilled Cheese

Daiya® cheddar and pepperjack cheese with tomatoes on local wheat bread. Add tempeh bacon for $1.50. Vegan.

PB&J

Peanut butter and mixed berry jelly on wheat bread. Try it pressed hot! Vegan.

Banana Fluffernutter

Peanut butter, banana and ricemellow fluff on local wheat bread. Pressed hot! Vegan.
